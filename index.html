Roulette (Money Mode)

American wheel: 0, 00, 1–36. You start with $100. Choose a bet amount, optionally guess a color, odd/even, or both.
Payouts: one correct guess → 2× bet payout. Both correct (parlay) → 4× bet payout. If you select both and only one is right, you lose the bet. 0 and 00 are green and are neither odd nor even.

Balance: $100
Last payout: $0
